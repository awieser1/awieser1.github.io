Andreas Wieser

I am currently a postdoc under the guidance of Elon Lindenstrauss and Shahar Mozes. Before that, I was a PhD student with Menny Aka and Manfred Einsiedler at ETH Zurich. My research concerns homogeneous dynamics and its application to problems in number theory. See for example this talk I gave at ICTS in Bangalore.
